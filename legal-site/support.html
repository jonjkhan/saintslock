Restore Purchases

If SaintsLock Premium does not appear after purchase, open the app and use the Restore Purchases button on the paywall or in Settings.

Cancel a Subscription

Manage or cancel your subscription through Apple:

Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SaintsLock

Troubleshooting Purchase Issues

Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase, then try Restore Purchases again. If the issue continues, contact support with a short description of what happened.

TestFlight Note

If you are using a TestFlight or beta build, some purchase or subscription behavior may differ from the public App Store release.
