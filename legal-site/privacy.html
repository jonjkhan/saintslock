SaintsLock

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

SaintsLock is an iOS app designed to support prayerful attention and spiritual discipline before opening distracting apps.

What SaintsLock Stores

SaintsLock stores basic settings, ritual progress, selected preferences, and usage statistics locally on your device so the app can remember your rule, track your prayer resets, and preserve your experience between sessions.

Subscriptions

SaintsLock uses RevenueCat for subscription management and purchase restoration. RevenueCat may process subscription-related information needed to verify purchases and determine Premium access.

Analytics

SaintsLock may add analytics later to help improve the product, understand feature quality, and reduce bugs. If analytics are added, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Future Screen Time Features

Future Apple Screen Time and Family Controls features may use Apple privacy-preserving tokens to represent selected apps or categories. SaintsLock does not need to know the full identity of every selected app in order to support those privacy-focused system features.

Data Sales

SaintsLock does not sell your personal data.

Important Limitations

SaintsLock is not a medical service, psychological service, or spiritual counseling service. The app is a personal discipline tool only.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, contact support@saintslock.app.
